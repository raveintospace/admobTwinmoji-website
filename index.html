About Twinmoji

Twinmoji is a fun and challenging game that tests your speed and sharpness! Can you spot the only repeated emoji between two cards? Play solo or battle a friend in two exciting modes:

Single Mode

Race against the clock to find the matching emoji across ten rounds. Earn points based on speed and difficulty, choose from four emoji decks, four countdown speeds, and four difficulty levels (3, 6, 9 or 12 emojis). Watch your score soar with every match!

Battle Mode

Challenge a friend in a head-to-head showdown. One player starts the countdown, the other races to find the repeated emoji. Score points for every win, but beware, mistakes cost you! First to 5 points wins, or lose at -5.

Features:

  • Smooth SwiftUI animations for an engaging experience.
  • Four unique emoji decks to keep the game fresh.
  • Customizable gameplay: Four speeds (Chill, Slow, Quick, Fast) and four difficulties (Easy, Medium, Advanced, Hard).
  • Color-based countdowns for intuitive timing.
  • Track your single high scores and challenge yourself.
  • Perfect for quick solo challenges or friendly competitions, Twinmoji brings emoji fun to your fingertips.
